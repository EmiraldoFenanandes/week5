Week 5 Activity

A megalopolis, sometimes called a megapolis; also megaregion, city cluster or supercity, is typically defined as a group of two or more roughly adjacent metropolitan area The megalopolis concept became highly influential as it introduced a new, larger scale in thinking about urban patterns and urban growth.
